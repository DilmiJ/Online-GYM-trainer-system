Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I sign up for online fitness training?

Signing up is easy! Just click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage and follow the instructions.

2. What equipment do I need for online workouts?

You don't need much equipment. Basic items like dumbbells, a yoga mat, and resistance bands can be useful, but many workouts can be done with just your body weight.

3. How do I pay for the online training programs?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards and PayPal. You can choose your preferred method during the signup process.

4. Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts, and you can do it from your account settings.

5. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team by emailing support@ymlb.com or by calling our toll-free number at 1-800-123-4567.
